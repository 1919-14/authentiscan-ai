The pipeline

Three transparent, explainable checks — no black-box model

Instead of a trained classifier, AuthentiScan AI combines lightweight, well-understood forensic signals that anyone can inspect and reason about.

Error Level Analysis

Re-compresses the image at a known JPEG quality and measures how unevenly each region "moves" — edited or synthetic regions compress differently from a genuine capture.

Noise-Consistency Check

Real camera sensors leave an organic, moderately-varying noise signature. Overly uniform or "too clean" local variance is a soft signal of AI generation or heavy smoothing.

EXIF Metadata Forensics

Checks for genuine camera-capture fields (Make, Model, exposure) versus missing metadata or software tags naming known generative-AI tools.
